iPhone SE Information

Overview

The iPhone SE is a smartphone designed, developed, and marketed by Apple Inc. It is a budget-friendly alternative to Apple's flagship iPhones, combining the design of the iPhone 8 with the performance of newer models. The iPhone SE was announced on April 15, 2020, and features a compact design, powerful A13 Bionic chip, and a single-camera system.

Key Features

  • 4.7-inch Retina HD display
  • A13 Bionic chip with third-generation Neural Engine
  • Single 12MP Wide camera with Portrait mode and Depth Control
  • Touch ID for secure authentication and Apple Pay
  • Wireless charging capability
  • iOS 13 with Dark Mode, new tools for editing photos and videos, and brand new privacy features

Specifications

Display 4.7-inch Retina HD LCD
Processor A13 Bionic chip
Storage Options 64GB, 128GB, 256GB
Camera Single 12MP Wide camera
Front Camera 7MP FaceTime HD camera
Operating System iOS 13 (upgradable to latest iOS version)

Price

The iPhone SE's price varies depending on the storage capacity. As of 2024, the prices typically range from $399 to $549.
