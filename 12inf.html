iPhone 12 Information

Overview

The iPhone 12 is a smartphone designed, developed, and marketed by Apple Inc. It is the fourteenth generation flagship iPhone, succeeding the iPhone 11. It was announced on October 13, 2020, and released on October 23, 2020. The iPhone 12 features a new flat-edge design, 5G support, and the powerful A14 Bionic chip.

Key Features

  • 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display
  • 5G support for faster download and streaming
  • A14 Bionic chip with next-generation Neural Engine
  • Dual-camera system with 12MP Ultra Wide and Wide cameras
  • Ceramic Shield front cover, tougher than any smartphone glass
  • MagSafe for easy attachment of accessories and faster wireless charging

Specifications

Display 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR OLED
Processor A14 Bionic chip
Storage Options 64GB, 128GB, 256GB
Camera Dual 12MP Ultra Wide and Wide cameras
Front Camera 12MP TrueDepth camera
Operating System iOS 14 (upgradable to latest iOS version)

Price

The iPhone 12's price varies depending on the storage capacity. As of 2024, the prices typically range from $799 to $1,099.
