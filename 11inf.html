iPhone 11 Information

Overview

The iPhone 11 is a smartphone designed, developed, and marketed by Apple Inc. It is the thirteenth generation lower-priced iPhone, succeeding the iPhone XR. It was unveiled on September 10, 2019, alongside the higher-end iPhone 11 Pro flagship at the Steve Jobs Theater in Apple Park, Cupertino, by Apple CEO Tim Cook.

Key Features

  • 6.1-inch Liquid Retina HD LCD display
  • Dual-camera system with 12MP Ultra Wide and Wide cameras
  • A13 Bionic chip with third-generation Neural Engine
  • Face ID for secure authentication and Apple Pay
  • Water and dust resistance (IP68)
  • iOS 13 with Dark Mode, new tools for editing photos and videos, and brand new privacy features

Specifications

Display 6.1-inch Liquid Retina HD LCD
Processor A13 Bionic chip
Storage Options 64GB, 128GB, 256GB
Camera Dual 12MP Ultra Wide and Wide cameras
Front Camera 12MP TrueDepth camera
Operating System iOS 13 (upgradable to iOS 14)

Price

The iPhone 11's price varies depending on the storage capacity. As of 2024, the prices typically range from $599 to $849.
