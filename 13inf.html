iPhone 13 Information

Overview

The iPhone 13 is a smartphone designed, developed, and marketed by Apple Inc. It is the fourteenth generation flagship iPhone, succeeding the iPhone 12. It was announced on September 14, 2021, alongside the iPhone 13 Mini, iPhone 13 Pro, and iPhone 13 Pro Max. The iPhone 13 features improved cameras, a more powerful A15 Bionic chip, and longer battery life.

Key Features

  • 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display
  • Improved dual-camera system with 12MP Wide and Ultra Wide cameras
  • A15 Bionic chip with next-generation Neural Engine
  • Advanced dual-camera system with Night mode, Deep Fusion, and Smart HDR 4
  • Ceramic Shield front cover, tougher than any smartphone glass
  • 5G support for faster download and streaming

Specifications

Display 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR OLED
Processor A15 Bionic chip
Storage Options 128GB, 256GB, 512GB
Camera Dual 12MP Wide and Ultra Wide cameras
Front Camera 12MP TrueDepth camera
Operating System iOS 15 (upgradable to latest iOS version)

Price

The iPhone 13's price varies depending on the storage capacity. As of 2024, the prices typically range from $799 to $1,399.
